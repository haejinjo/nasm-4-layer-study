## Content
### Multiplanar Lunge to Two-Arm Dumbbell Press

---

#### TECHNIQUE

When performing any squatting or lunging motion, keep the foot straight and align the knees with the second and third toes. This ensures proper joint mechanics and optimal force generation, increasing the benefit of the exercise while decreasing its risk.
