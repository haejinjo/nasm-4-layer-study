## Content
### Single-Leg Balance

---

#### TECHNIQUE

Make sure the gluteal musculature of the balance leg remains contracted while performing this and all balance exercises to help stabilize the lower extremity.

### Single-Leg Balance Reach

---

#### TECHNIQUE

Keep the hips level when performing balance exercises. This will improve balance and decrease stress to the low-back.
