## Content
### Single-Leg Hip Rotation

---

#### TECHNIQUE

Make sure when performing this exercise to rotate through the hip of the balance leg versus the spine. This will decrease stress to the spine and enhance control of the core.

### Single-Leg Lift and Chop

---

#### TECHNIQUE

When performing balance exercises, make sure the knee of the balance leg always remains aligned with the toes.
