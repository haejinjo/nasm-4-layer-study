# Resistance Training Exercises

The following is a list of common stabilization-focused, strength-focused, and power-focused resistance training exercises. However, this is not an exhaustive list.

## Total-Body Exercise Descriptions

### Ball Squat, Curl to Press

---

#### TECHNIQUE

When performing any form of a ball squat, try to use the ball to guide one through the squatting motion (sitting in a chair) versus relying on the ball for support (leaning back on the ball).
