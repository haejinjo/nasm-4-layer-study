## Content
## Cable Rotation

### TECHNIQUE

To decrease stress to the low-back, make sure to pivot the back leg into triple extension:

	- Hip extension

	- Knee extension

	- Ankle plantarflexion (extension)

This also ensures proper coordination of the muscles that extend the lower extremities (gluteus maximus, quadriceps, and calves).

## Cable Lift

### TECHNIQUE

Do not hyperextend the low-back at the top position. Keep a neutral spine throughout. Like the cable rotation, the back leg should pivot into triple extension at the end of the movement.
